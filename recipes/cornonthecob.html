Corn on the Cob

Description

Corn on the cob tastes good when made correctly. Today's corn on the cob uses genetically modified plant material to bring us the delicious vegetable grain organic thing we have today. It tastes very good with butter and seasoning too.

Ingredients

  • 6 ears corn
  • 6 tablespoons butter, softened
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate.
  2. Peel back corn husks and remove silk. Place 1 tablespoon butter, salt and pepper on each piece of corn. Close husks.
  3. Wrap each ear of corn tightly in aluminum foil.
  4. Cook on the preheated grill, turning occasionally, until tender, about 30 minutes.
